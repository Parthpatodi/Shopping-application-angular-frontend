product designing


The fabric of choice for product is chiffon, georgette, net or crepe. Chiffon is the most common fabric used for making lehengas. The more modern types of product material include georgette, chiffon and crepe. They are usually made of silk though artificial fabrics like rayon can also be used. Georgette and chiffon are known for their soft, smooth and feminine feel while crepe has a unique crumpled texture.
